Help with the prior import consent form

You must have a PIC form before submitting a firearms export licence application.

If a country does not class a certain weapon as a firearm, a PIC form may not be issued, and therefore an end-user undertaking form (EUU) would be sufficient. It must be submitted together with a letter or email from the relevant importing countries licensing authorities stating that a PIC form or equivalent cannot be issued. You use the 'Additional information' section to upload any supporting documents.
