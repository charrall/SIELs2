Upload a prior import consent

You must attach a prior import consent, or an explanation if one is not yet available or needed. If the document is not in English you must provide a translation.

In countries where a weapon is not considered to be a firearm,an import consent is not issued. In this instance, an end-user undertaking form (EUU) is required. It must be submitted together with a letter or email from the relevant importing countries licensing authorities stating that a PIC or equivalent cannot be issued.

The file must be smaller than 50MB.
