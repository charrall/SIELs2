Help with the prior import consent form

You must have PIC before submitting a firearms export licence application.

Many countries do not class some weapons to be firearms so a PIC form is not issued. In this instance, an end-user undertaking form (EUU) is sufficient. It must be submitted together with a letter or email from the relevant importing countries licensing authorities stating that a PIC or equivalent cannot be issued.
